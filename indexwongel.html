My Story

My name is Wongelawit, and I am a student. I am an 11th-grade student at Edget Chorra Secondary School in Addis Ababa, Lemi Kura Sub-City.

I was born and raised in Addis Ababa. I studied grades 1 and 2 at Bole BirhanEzare Primary School, grade 3 at Addis Gebeya Birhane Helina School, grades 4 and 5 at Saris Abo Bulbula School, grades 6 and 7 at Figa Bole Addis School, and completed grade 8 at Jefe First Level School. Currently, I am studying here from grades 9 to 11.

As seen from my background, I have lived in various places, attended different types of schools, and experienced different learning processes. Likewise, I have gone through different stages of life. Through it all, I have witnessed God's goodness, and I have endured with inner strength and perseverance. I believe that my future will also be good by the grace of God!
